About This Trip

Why Nagoya?

I visited Nagoya on June 12, 2026, and wanted to put together this website as a record of the trip. Nagoya isn't always the first city people think of when it comes to Japan, but it has a great mix of history, food, and everyday city life that made it worth exploring.

This site covers where I stayed, the places I visited, the food I tried, and a day-by-day diary of the trip. Feel free to look around the different pages using the navigation menu above.

About Me

I'm a student who enjoys traveling and trying new food whenever I get the chance. This trip to Nagoya was a short but memorable one.

Trip Overview

One day trip to Nagoya, Japan, focused on visiting the castle, trying local food, and exploring the city center.
