Trip Diary

June 12, 2026 - A Day in Nagoya

June 12, 2026
by: Ashidbileg Erdenejav

I started the day early, heading straight for Nagoya Castle. The walk up to the main grounds was pleasant, and the castle itself, with its golden roof ornaments, was worth the visit. I spent some time in the museum inside learning about the history of the building before moving on.

After the castle, I made my way to Osu Shopping District for lunch. This is where I tried hitsumabushi for the first time, and it quickly became the highlight of the trip food-wise. The area itself was full of small shops and side streets that were fun to wander through.

I ended the day at Atsuta Shrine, which felt like a completely different atmosphere compared to the busy streets of Osu. It was quiet, shaded, and a good way to wind down before heading back.
